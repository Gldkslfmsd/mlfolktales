Story Title: The He-goats and She-goats
Story ID (from ATU-website): 7995
ATU Classification Number: UNKNOWN

When the She-Goats from Jove obtain'dA beard, th' indignant Males complain'd,That females by this near approachWould on their gravity encroach."Suffer, my sapient friends," says he,"Their eminence in this degree,And bear their beard's most graceful length,As they can never have your strength."Warn'd by this little tale, agreeWith men in gen'ral form'd like theeWhile you by virtue still exceed,And in the spirit take the lead.
