Story Title: The Hungry Dogs
Story ID (from ATU-website): 7957
ATU Classification Number: UNKNOWN

A stupid plan that fools project,Not only will not take effect,ut proves destructive in the endTo those that bungle and pretend.Some hungry Dogs beheld an hideDeep sunk beneath the crystal tide,Which, that they might extract for food,They strove to drink up all the flood;But bursten in the desp'rate deed,They perish'd, ere they could succeed.
